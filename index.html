About Me

Biography

My life changed when I received a DSLR last year as an engagement present. With that, I decided not to be that guy with the fancy toy that runs on auto. So I enrolled at Brooklyn Central, an awesome little photo school in Dumbo. Further curiosity took me to Photoshop, and then web development. Plans to flee the corporate world ensued. I am now learning the MEAN stack in an immersive course at Fullstack Academy in NYC, while earning some cash shooting photos. It's all gonna work -- stay tuned!
